What we offer

Equine therapy, or horse assisted therapy, is a type of psychotherapy which incorporates horses into the therapeutic process. You will engage in activities such as grooming, feeding, leading and even riding a horse while being supervised by one of our mental health professionals. The sessions revolve around subjects such as trust and empathy, impulse control, emotional awareness, confidence, social-skills and problem solving skills.
